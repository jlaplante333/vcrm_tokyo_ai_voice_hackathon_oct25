CRMblr Privacy Policy

We're committed to protecting your data and being transparent about our practices.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

What Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a CRMblr account, we collect your name, email address, organization name, and contact details.

CRM Data

You upload and manage your organization's data. This is your data—we simply provide the platform to organize it.

Usage Information

We collect basic information about how you use CRMblr to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: We use your information to provide and maintain CRMblr's features.
  • Customer Support: Your information helps us respond to your requests and provide assistance.
  • Communication: We use your contact information to send important service updates.

How We Share Your Information

Your Organization

Your CRM data is accessible to authorized users within your organization.

We Don't Sell Your Data: CRMblr will never sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law.

Data Security & Retention

Security Measures

We implement standard security measures including encryption and access controls.

Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. You can delete your data anytime.

Your Rights

  • Access & Export: You can access and export your data anytime.
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and data by contacting us.
  • Data Portability: Export your data in standard formats (CSV, Excel, PDF).
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through your account settings.

Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? We're here to help.

This policy is designed to be clear and understandable. If you need clarification, please reach out.
